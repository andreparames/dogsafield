Discover dog-friendly events and meetups happening near you. Dogs Afield connects you with a local community of dog owners for walks, park hangs, and outdoor adventures.

Features:
- See nearby dog events on an interactive map
- Host your own walks, meetups, and playdates
- RSVP to events and let other owners know you're coming
- Verify connections after meeting up — build your local pack
- Message other dog owners directly

You and your dog deserve more than the same walk every day. Explore what's happening around you.
