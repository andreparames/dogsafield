# 📦 The Anchor Host Playbook: Saturday Morning Pack Walks
Thank you for partnering with us to host the weekly Austin Pack Walks! Your goal is to create a safe, welcoming, and recurring weekly ritual for local dog owners.
As our **Anchor Host**, you are the face of our community on the ground. We handle the RSVPs, marketing, and coordination remotely; you focus on what you do best—leading a safe, happy pack.
## 🛠️ Prep Checklist (Do This Before the Walk)
 * **The Route:** Establish a flat, shade-heavy, 30-to-45-minute loop (ideally in a pedestrian-friendly area like Zilker, Mueller, or East Austin). It must start and end at a spot with easy parking.
 * **The Gear:** Bring a basic pet first-aid kit, extra poop bags, and a spare slip lead just in case.
 * **The "Welcome Pack":** Grab a bag of premium, allergy-friendly dog treats (reimbursed by us) to hand out to attendees.
## 🕒 The 3-Step Event Flow
```
  [10 Mins: Meet & Greet] ➔ [30 Mins: The Walk] ➔ [5 Mins: Photos & Wrap]

```
### Phase 1: The Meet & Greet (10 Mins)
As people arrive at the starting point, greet them and set the ground rules.
 1. **Welcome Owners:** *"Hi! I'm [Name], your host today. Welcome to the weekly Pack Walk!"*
 2. **Enforce the "3-Foot Rule":** Keep all dogs on a 3-foot leash. Ensure there are no nose-to-nose greetings immediately. Dogs need space to settle their energy.
 3. **Prep the Pack:** Explain the route and tell the group: *"We walk as a pack, but keep a respectful distance between dogs so everyone feels safe."*
### Phase 2: The Walk (30 Mins)
Lead the group at a steady, casual pace.
 * **Pace Setter:** Walk at the front to guide the route, but occasionally look back to ensure the group isn't stretching out too far.
 * **Facilitate the Humans:** Use the walk to chat with the owners. Ask about their dogs, how long they've lived in Austin, etc.
 * **Active Monitoring:** Keep an eye out for dog body language (stiff tails, lip-curling, panting heavily from the Austin heat).
### Phase 3: The Wrap-Up & Photos (5 Mins)
End the walk back at the starting point with a high-value moment.
 1. **The "Puppy Paparazzi" (Crucial):** Gather everyone for a quick group photo. Ask the owners to hold their dogs' attention with a treat while you take **3 to 5 clear, high-quality photos/videos** of the dogs looking at the camera.
 2. **Hand Out Treats:** Distribute the treats to the owners to give to their dogs as a reward for being "good citizens."
 3. **The Plug:** Remind them to come back next week: *"Thanks for coming! We do this every Saturday. Keep an eye on your inbox/calendar for next week's invite!"*
## ⚠️ Safety & Weather Protocols (Strict)
> 🌡️ **The Austin Heat Rule:** If the temperature is projected to be above **90°F (32°C)** during the walk, the event must be moved to an earlier time (e.g., 8:00 AM) or rescheduled. Paw pads burn easily on hot asphalt!
> 
 * **Scuffles:** If a dog becomes over-stimulated or reactive, calmly ask the owner to step to the back of the pack to give their dog a 10-foot buffer.
 * **Hydration:** Always carry water and a collapsible bowl. Offer water breaks halfway through the walk.
## 💰 How to Get Paid
Within **2 hours** of the event ending, please send the following to your community coordinator:
 1. **The Photos:** Send your 3–5 best group photos/videos.
 2. **The Headcount:** Confirm the final number of humans and dogs who attended.
 3. **Your Invoice:** We will immediately process your **$75 payment** + treat reimbursement via your preferred payment method (Venmo, PayPal, or Stripe).
